About

I'm Jorge Ayala, a Toronto-based Digital Media Professional. I specialize in web development, social media and web content management, photography, video production, sound design and installation art.

I completed my undergraduate studies in New Media at the School of Image Arts at Ryerson University and have participated in workshops at IRCAM (Paris), Interaccess (Canada), and the Experimental Graphics Workshop (Havana, Cuba). I currently works as a freelance web developer, and as a video technical programmer at the Ryerson Image Centre.
